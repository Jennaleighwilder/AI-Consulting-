    Architecture
    Constitutional layer

    West-OS is the constitutional layer of a larger AI governance architecture — the part that decides, audits, and escalates before action leaves the trust boundary. Four sacred systems form the spine; details stay under governance until you are cleared for the full map.

    West-OS

    Governor, engines, evidence packets — threat intelligence and ALLOW / DEFER / REVIEW / DENY at the edge.

    GAIA

    Grounding and environmental truth — how the stack knows what “real world” constraints apply before models speak.

    Mirror OS

    Reflection and alignment memory — continuity of policy and self-consistency across sessions and agents.

    Nyx

    Dark-spectrum operations — containment, deferred disclosure, and what happens when the public surface must stay quiet.

    There is more beneath these four names — enough to warrant a second conversation.
